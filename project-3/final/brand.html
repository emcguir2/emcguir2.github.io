Design Research


In my design research class in the Fall of 2017, my class worked together with the city of South Bend to research and discuss plans for an Inclusive Technology Resource Center. We worked with local businesses, residents, and other organizations to discuss what was the best manifestation of this resource. I was a part of the Branding team, that eventaully decided to suggest South Bend Spark, as a center with ties to its "rust belt" identity and the growth that is occuring.
