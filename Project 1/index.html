Neue Kabel / Regular

Neue Kabel is Marc Shutz’s updated version of the original design. Neue Kabel maintains many of the original features and the charm of the Kabel font and modernizes it with minor tweaks. The geometric sans serif, designed in the 1920s was initially designed for a German Museum. Shutz’s goal in updating the typeface was “to create forms that perform well in modern imaging environments while keeping the character and charm of Koch’s design.”
